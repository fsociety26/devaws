Solutions for Infrastructure Problems

Bad Roads: Regular maintenance, use of durable materials, and public-private partnerships for development.

Lack of Public Transport: Improve local transport systems, promote metro/bus projects, and ensure affordability.

Water Scarcity: Rainwater harvesting, check dams, and awareness for water conservation.

Electricity Issues: Invest in renewable energy sources and improve distribution infrastructure.
