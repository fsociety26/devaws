Solutions for Crime and Safety Issues

Theft and Robbery: Improve street lighting, install CCTV cameras, and increase police patrolling.

Domestic Violence: Set up helplines, women shelters, and run awareness campaigns.

Cyber Crime: Educate people about digital safety, strengthen cybersecurity laws, and increase digital literacy.

Corruption: Promote transparency, encourage digital transactions, and implement strong anti-corruption laws.
