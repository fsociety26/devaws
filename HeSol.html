Solutions for Health Issues

Malnutrition: Implement mid-day meal schemes, provide nutrition education, and promote balanced diets.

Lack of Medical Facilities: Build more hospitals and clinics, especially in rural areas; increase healthcare budgets.

Drug Addiction: Spread awareness, open rehabilitation centers, and support counseling services.

Mental Health Issues: Introduce mental health education, provide accessible counseling, and reduce stigma.
